CheckBank overview

CheckBank is your online resource to help you make sound investment decisions and avoid fraud.

Our specialists also research and provide information on a range of topics, such as whether a broker or investment professional is properly licensed to do business, or whether a company is officially registered.

Moreover, we provide information of a wide variability of banks in your region with their respective promotions for a loan or a saving. This includes obviously all the interest rates and types of interest that they provide.

We cannot tell investors what investments to make, but we can provide them with unbiased information on investment decisions and on protecting themselves from securities fraud or abuse.

Values: The way we do things

Our core values are Customer First, Humility, Passion, Integrity, and Boundarylessness. Values guide our actions and define the way we work. We encourage all our colleagues to exemplify and role model the Values.

  • Customer First: Keep customers at the center of everything we do.
  • Humility: Be open to learn and change.
  • Passion: Demonstrate infectious energy to win and excel.
  • Integrity: Do the right thing.
  • Boundarylessness: Treat organisation agenda as paramount.
